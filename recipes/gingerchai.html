Ginger Chai

Description

Ginger chai, another favourite beverage of mine! To prepare ginger chai, black tea is steeped in a blend of milk and water and then sweetened with sugar, while the infusion of ginger imparts the unique flavor profile of ginger chai.

Ingredients

  • Water 1/4 cup
  • Milk 1/2 cup
  • Black Tea Leaves 3 tsp or Black Tea Powder 1 tsp
  • Sugar 1 tsp
  • Ginger 0.5 in

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil
  2. Add the tea leaves/powder with the ginger and let it brew for 3-5 minutes on medium flame
  3. Pour the milk into the pot and let it heat to desired heat
  4. Add the sugar to the pot and mix it in
  5. Strain it into a cup and enjoy it hot with some biscuits!
