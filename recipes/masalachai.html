Masala Chai

Description

Masala chai, my favourite beverage and a beloved beverage in South Asia and, has its origins in the early modern Indian subcontinent. To prepare chai, black tea is steeped in a blend of milk and water and then sweetened with sugar, while the infusion of aromatic herbs and spices imparts the unique flavor profile of masala chai.

Ingredients

  • Water 1/4 cup
  • Milk 1/2 cup
  • Black Tea Leaves 3 tsp or Black Tea Powder 1 tsp
  • Sugar 1 tsp
  • Cardamom 1 pod
  • Clove 1 whole
  • Peppercorns 2 whole

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil
  2. Add the tea leaves/powder with the spices and let it brew for 3-5 minutes on medium flame
  3. Pour the milk into the pot and let it heat to desired heat
  4. Add the sugar to the pot and mix it in
  5. Strain it into a cup and enjoy it hot with some biscuits!
