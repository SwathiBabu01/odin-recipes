Ginger Chai

Description

Black tea is prepared by steeping tea in water and then sweetened with sugar, and the flavor can be enhanced by using honey and ginger.

Ingredients

  • Water 1.25 cup
  • Honey or Sugar 1 tsp
  • Black Tea Leaves 3 tsp or Black Tea Powder 1 tsp
  • Ginger 0.5 in (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil
  2. Add the tea leaves/powder with the ginger and let it brew for 3-5 minutes on medium flame
  3. Add the sugar/honey to the pot and mix it in
  4. Strain it into a cup and enjoy!
